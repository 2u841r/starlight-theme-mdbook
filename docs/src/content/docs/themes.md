---
title: Themes
description: The 5 color schemes included with starlight-theme-mdbook.
---

Click the paintbrush icon in the navbar to switch between these at any
time — the choice is saved per-visitor, same as mdBook's own theme popup.

## Light

mdBook's default light scheme: white page, near-black sidebar text on a
soft gray sidebar background.

## Dark

mdBook's "Coal" scheme: near-black page and sidebar, muted blue-gray text.

## Rust

The scheme most people associate with "the Rust Book" — a warm cream page
paired with a dark brown sidebar and orange accents.

## Valentine

Ported from [daisyUI](https://daisyui.com)'s Valentine theme: a soft rose
page with a dark rose sidebar and bright pink accents.

## Caramellatte

Also ported from daisyUI: a warm cream page with a terracotta sidebar and
brown/amber accents.

:::tip[Auto]
There's also an "Auto" option in the picker that follows the visitor's OS
light/dark preference (mapped to Light or Dark above).
:::
